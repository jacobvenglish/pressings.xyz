About Pressings.xyz
Pressings.xyz

Built By A Collector

Pressings.xyz is a personal archive of physical music packaging. It was built by Your Name, a designer and collector based in Your City, who has spent years accumulating records, CDs, and cassettes — and paying as much attention to the sleeve as to the music inside it.

This started as a personal reference library. Every time a record arrived with particularly considered typography, an unexpected print finish, or a gatefold that opened to reveal something worth studying — it went into a folder. Eventually that folder became a system. The system became this site.

The goal is to be the resource that didn't exist when I started learning about packaging design — a proper archive that credits the designers, documents the production specs, and treats the sleeve as seriously as the music.

What this is

01 Not a database

Discogs is a database. It catalogues what exists. Pressings.xyz is an archive — it studies how things were made. Every entry is scanned by hand, tagged with production details, and credited to the designers and photographers who made it. The focus is always the packaging as a designed object.

02 A design resource

The archive is built to be useful to working designers. Every entry includes print finish, packaging type, and any special production features. The Formats section is a full technical reference for anyone designing physical music packaging. Designer credits are searchable and cross-referenced across labels, eras, and aesthetics.
