Curator Notes 04

The i,i cassette is a considered packaging object — the orange and green outer slip case with its dual spine construction creates an unusual unboxing experience before you even reach the inner jewel case. Aaron Anderson and Eric Timothy Carlson's artwork for the J-card is beautifully abstract and minimal — painterly without being decorative. Their visual language for Bon Iver consistently treats the sleeve as a mood rather than a statement.
