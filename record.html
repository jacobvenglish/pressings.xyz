Curator Notes 04

The "six-eye" label variant is one of the most recognisable in jazz collecting — the six small "eye" logos arranged around the label edge are a reliable indicator of an early pressing. Neil Fujita's cover for Kind of Blue is a masterclass in restraint: the blue wash, the single portrait photograph, the clean serif type. Nothing competes. The design serves the music without comment.
