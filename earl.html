Curator Notes 04

Art directed by Sage Elsesser and Jakobi McLemore with album photography by Julien Wolf. The royal blue, tan, and black palette is bold and deliberate — the image-traced illustration style throughout gives the packaging a raw, high-contrast energy that feels consistent with Earl's aesthetic. The combination of script and sans-serif type across the J-card shows real typographic consideration for a cassette format.
