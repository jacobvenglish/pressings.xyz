About Pressings.xyz

Design
Lives
In Detail

Pressings.xyz is a personal archive of physical music packaging built by a collector training to be a packaging designer. Every entry is scanned by hand, tagged with care, and catalogued with attention to the designers, art directors, photographers, and print studios behind the work.

The sleeve. The fold. The label. The paper weight. The ink. These things are not accidents.

What is archived

  • 01High-resolution scans of every surface — front, back, spine, gatefold, inner sleeve, booklet, label, disc, cassette shell
  • 02Design credits — art director, designer, photographer, illustrator — wherever they can be found
  • 03Print production details — finish, packaging type, special features, paper stock
  • 04Precise metadata — label, catalogue number, country, year of this specific pressing
  • 05Aesthetic tagging for visual research and reference
Paid Access

Free browsing for everyone. Paid access unlocks full-resolution scans, complete booklet spreads, and downloadable assets — built for working designers and art directors.
